Casual Poker Games that I like!!
    Hold em
  • Start with 2 cards each
  • 5 cards down on table
  • Bet, Flip and bet for each 3(flop),1(turn),1(river)
  • High only
    Star Wars (Also Omaha Hi-Low Split)
  • Start with 5 cards each
  • 5 cards down on table
  • Use exactly 2 cards from hand
  • Flip 3(flop),1(turn),1(river) with a bet after each
  • High/Low declare and bet
  • Normally, A-2-3-4-6 best low
  • Ignore straight/flush for low variation
  • 8 or better can't do low unless all 5 (played) cards are 8 or under
    Money game
  • 2 cards each, 3 cards up plus deck in middle
  • Cards cost .25,.50,.75 and 1.00 for down deck
  • Each person "buys" a card from one of four piles
  • First player to get cards each round moves around table
  • Continue until everyone has 7 cards, replace cards till bet
  • Replace last card after each round bet (Do not replace last card if up card taken before a betting round)
  • Remaining up cards are community cards
  • High/Low
    Michigan
  • 5 cards hand, 5 on table
  • Flip one at a time
  • High-low point game
  • Ace 1 or 11, 10 is 10 or zero, 5 is 5 or -5
    Mexico
  • Start with 2 cards each
  • Dealer takes 4 cards in hand
  • Offer card to each
  • First two players may pass card, third must take card
  • Betting round
  • Repeat until everyone has 7 cards
  • High/Low
    Bingo
  • 6 cards hand flip one up on deck
  • High-low point game
  • Ace 1 or 11, pictures 10
  • Wait for turn to throw one card
  • Add one card from hand
  • All others throw that card
